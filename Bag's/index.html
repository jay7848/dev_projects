About Us

We make brands that break out, break through and break open.

It was still early days for the outdoors and the adventure industry in the country. Quality equipment remained a distant dream for most Indians. Spotting the opportunity, they channelled their passion for the outdoors into a company that would make international quality gear accessible to India’s growing outdoor community. Be it outdoor-friendly clothing, ergonomically designed
rucksacks or all-terrain footwear, Wildcraft has one mission – to engineer versatile, multi-functional, light-weight, weather-resilient products for the savvy backpacker; that enables people’s exploration of the outdoors.
